Our Services

Production

In most of our factories production is partially automated while otherrs being done by our casual labourers. Our casual labourers whom we hire on daily basis helps us in the seiving of sand, so as to produce a softer sand for use. So as to produce a high quality product.

Production Process

After that our casual labourers do the weighing of our materials that is : Sand and the Preservative Chemicals since Cements are brought in bags already accurately measured. After that is when automation comes in where the materials are put in a Mixer machine by the labourers for production.

Packaging

Packaging Process

After the production of the dry construction concrete, we package it in bags that are easy to handle and transport.

Our packaging process ensures that the products remain intact and are protected from environmental factors during transit.

The packaging is done in small 25kg bags that are mannually done by labourers who mannually put the dry conrete in the bags and sew the mouth of the bag using the electrical sewing machine.

After successful packaging, the bags are stored in our warehouses until they are ready for distribution.
